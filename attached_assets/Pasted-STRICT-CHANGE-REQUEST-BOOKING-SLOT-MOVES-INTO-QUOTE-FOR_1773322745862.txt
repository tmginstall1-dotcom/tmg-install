- When a customer submits a quote using a slot, that slot becomes unavailable to other customers immediately

3) HOLD / CONFLICT LOGIC

- On successful quote submission, selected slot becomes HELD for that quote
- HELD slots are hidden from other customers immediately
- When deposit is paid, HELD becomes CONFIRMED (or RESERVED_PENDING_ADMIN_CONFIRMATION if that matches current workflow better)
- If quote is cancelled, rejected, expired, or deposit is not paid within the configured hold window, release the slot automatically
- Use a sensible default hold window, e.g. 2 hours or admin-configurable if easy
- Add server-side protection against race conditions so two customers cannot get the same slot
- Do not rely on frontend-only checks; enforce availability in database/server logic
